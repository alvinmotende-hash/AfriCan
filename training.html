Training Overview

AfriCan training sessions are designed to build leadership, communication, and innovation skills. Each session lasts one hour and takes place twice a week. Our goal is to provide accessible, impactful mentorship across Africa.

Weekly Schedule

Sessions are available at different times to accommodate mentors and mentees:

  • Morning: 9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
  • Afternoon: 2:00 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Evening: 7:00 PM – 8:00 PM
